How to Play

Football Guessr is a daily puzzle: work out the mystery footballer from the clubs they played for.

What am I trying to do?

Name the mystery footballer. You are shown clubs from their career — one to start — and you guess who it is.

How does a round work?

Type a footballer's name and press Guess. Every wrong guess reveals the next club in the mystery player's career, giving you more to go on. You have 25 guesses — name the player to win, or the game ends once you run out.

What is the “career path”?

The clubs revealed so far, listed in the order the player joined them. The newest reveal is tagged Latest. Your wrong guesses are listed alongside it, so you never repeat one by accident.

Daily vs Practice — what's the difference?

Daily is one puzzle per day, the same for everyone and numbered like a certain word game. Your progress is saved, so you can close the tab and pick the same puzzle back up later.

Practice gives you a fresh random player whenever you like. It isn't saved mid-game, and a result is only recorded once the game finishes.

Do I have to spell the name exactly?

No. Guesses ignore capitalisation and accents and tolerate small spelling slips — for example, mbappe matches Mbappé.

Can I share my result?

Yes. When a game ends you can share a spoiler-free grid — a red square for each wrong guess and a green one for the correct answer — along with your score, like Daily #12 — 4/25. It never reveals the player's name.

Where are my stats?

The profile icon in the top-right corner tracks your win rate, current and best streaks, and how many guesses you typically need — separately for Daily and Practice.
